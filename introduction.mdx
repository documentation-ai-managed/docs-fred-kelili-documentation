## Best Practices

You follow these guidelines to maximize the value of your documentation space.

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="Content Organization" default-open="true">
    Structure your documentation with clear categories and logical hierarchies. Use consistent naming conventions and maintain a table of contents for easy navigation.
  </Expandable>
  <Expandable title="Version Control" default-open="false">
    Leverage version history to track changes and maintain documentation accuracy. Create backups regularly and establish review processes for critical updates.
  </Expandable>
  <Expandable title="Accessibility" default-open="false">
    Ensure your documentation is accessible to all team members. Use clear language, provide alt text for images, and maintain consistent formatting.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

<Callout kind="tip">Start small with your most frequently accessed information, then expand your documentation coverage gradually.</Callout>
